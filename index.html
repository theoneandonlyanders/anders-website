Bio:
Anders Vestergaard is a drummer, composer and improviser. Anders has toured worldwide and is a part of the bands Yes Deer, Baujillaud, Jesper Zeuthen / Jacob Anderskov / Anders Vestergaard, Blomsten and Laser Nun and has more than 40 releases on different labels. He also works as a solo artist and has released 3 solo albums (Eel 2016, Prime Float/Unitary Perfect 2019 and Propeller 2024). Besides his steady bands he has collaborated with artists such as Evan Parker, Guro Moe, Marcela Lucatelli, and Taku Unami, and he has performed at festivals such as Sound of Stockholm, Roskilde Festival, Taktlos Festival, AMR Festival, MÃ³zg Festival, All Ears, Saalfelden Jazz Festival etc. He also writes music for Crush String Collective and runs the label Abstract Tits together with Aron Dahl.
